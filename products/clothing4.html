Midnight Grace

This refined look blends classic charm with modern elegance, creating an effortlessly polished appearance. The blush pink blouse, adorned with a crisp white Peter Pan collar and delicate puff sleeves, adds a touch of soft femininity. Paired with the black and grey vintage midi dress, the lace-up bodice provides a structured, corset-like silhouette, while the flowing grey skirt adds graceful movement. The black T-strap heeled shoes, featuring a bow embellishment and a sturdy block heel, enhance the vintage aesthetic while ensuring comfort. For accessories, the black tote bag with its monochrome scarf detail introduces a sophisticated contrast, while the gold bow and pearl stud earrings bring a delicate sparkle. Completing the look, the mint green quilted handbag with its pearl handle and gold-tone chain offers a charming pop of colour, balancing the muted tones with an air of refined luxury.
