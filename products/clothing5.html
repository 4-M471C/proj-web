Blossom & Cherries

This charming look blends soft pastels with classic details, creating a refined yet lively appearance. The mint green buttoned playsuit, with its structured fit and sweetheart neckline, provides a fresh and flattering silhouette, while the front button detailing adds a subtle vintage charm. Layered over it, the chiffon cherry print petticoat introduces a delicate touch with its sheer fabric and vibrant cherry pattern, perfect for a light and breezy feel. The vintage-inspired T-strap heels in mint and white enhance the colour palette, their scalloped edges and bow details offering a playful yet polished finish. The elegant white satin clutch bag, adorned with a rhinestone clasp, complements the outfit with a hint of luxury, while the retro cat-eye sunglasses in mint green add a stylish and dramatic edge. Tying everything together, the gold bow and pearl stud earrings bring a touch of refinement, completing the look with understated elegance.
